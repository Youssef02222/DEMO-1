Mission & Vision

mission of MQS at medical laboratories is to ensure that laboratory results are accurate, reliable, and meet the required quality standards and guidelines. This includes implementing and maintaining a quality management system, implementing quality control measures, maintaining accurate records, regularly evaluating laboratory performance, providing ongoing training and education to laboratory personnel, and ensuring excellent customer service.

The vision of MQS in medical laboratories is to achieve a culture of continuous improvement and excellence in laboratory operations, ultimately leading to the highest quality of patient care. Our company program aims to establish and maintain a system of procedures, processes, and activities that promote a culture of excellence, innovation, continuous learning and Consistent adherence to regulatory requirements.
